Bapuji Educational Association (BEA) is a conglomerate of over 50 educational institutions across the city of Davangere.

Under-Graduate

BIET offers Bachelor of Engineering (B.E) in 10 disciplines like Civil, Mechanical, Electrical & Electronics, Electronics & Communication, Textile Technology, Bio-Technology, Chemical, Electronics & Instrumentation, Computer Science & Engineering and Information Science & Engineering.

Post-Graduate

BIET offers Four Post Graduate programs like MBA, MCA and M.Tech. in Environmental Engineering and Structural Engineering.

Research Programs

BIET provides a conducive environment to carry out quality Research, hence offers Research in 13 disciplines, which are recognized by reputed VTU and Kuvempu University.
